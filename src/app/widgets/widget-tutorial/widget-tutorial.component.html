Getting started

Welcome to KIP! This text is a widget on a page. Pages are "Locked" by default so a stray touch can't change your layout. Open the KIP Help documentation to learn more.

Two things are always within reach:

  • The toolbar — a bar at the top that stays hidden until you need it. It holds Settings, fullscreen, night mode, notifications, the edit button, and the page navigator. Reveal it by swiping down from the top edge, scrolling up with a mouse or trackpad, or tapping the thin strip that peeks at the top. It hides again when you scroll back, tap elsewhere, or leave it idle.
  • The alarm badge — when an alarm is active, a notification button appears in the lower-left corner and stays there until the alarm clears. Tap it to see the alarms.

Tip: on a locked page, press and hold a data widget to open its history plot directly.

Sign in to Signal K

Skip signs you in through your Signal K server session — there is no separate Skip password. If the server requires authentication, go to KIP's Connectivity options and click Sign in to be taken to the server's sign-in page; once you are signed in the browser returns here. Then click the upper right X button to get back to this page (Connectivity options).

  • If you don’t have a Signal K account, ask your captain or access the Signal K Admin Settings / Users page and create a user.
  • If sign-in fails, make sure your device is on the same network as your Signal K server and that you are reaching Skip through the server itself.

Changing layouts

While a page is unlocked, page navigation is paused so drags don't flip pages. Follow these steps to learn how to change your page layout:
  1. Resize this Tutorial widget using the now visible dashed borders or corner arrows. Resize it so it does not take up all the screen. You need free space on your page to add more widgets. You can delete this Tutorial widget later when you are done with the instructions.
  2. Tap (or click) an empty area and choose Add Widget to open the selection panel.
  3. Choose a widget from the selection panel to add it to the page.

Working with a widget

  • Touch and drag to move widgets
  • Drag corners and sides to resize widgets
  • Tap a widget to open its action menu — configure, duplicate, delete, copy, or cut it

Leaving edit mode

Use the Save or Cancel buttons in the lower right corner to either persist your changes or revert changes.

Adding and managing pages

Reveal the toolbar and open the Settings button. From there you can add, rename, order, delete, and duplicate entire pages.
